Rating Description
Staples Amazing, top tier general staple across multiple decks
Deck Staples Core cards in particular deck archetypes
Good Good cards not currently in core of popular decks, or previous deck staples
Former Cards that used to be deck staples, but fallen out of favour. May return in the future.
Casual Cards great for limited collections (new players), not really seen in top tier decks
Deck Enabler Specialised cards, not essential to certain deck archetypes, but can add in
Meh Cards that don't see play, kinda mediocre
Meme Fun cards, not so much for competitive decks
Bad Not so great cards, but who knows, in the future. Dust at your own risk.
Trash Will never be good, or easily replacable. Dust.
